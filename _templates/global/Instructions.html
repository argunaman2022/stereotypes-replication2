This study consists of two parts.
  1. First, you will play a Memory game. You will have 2 minutes to score as many points as you can in this game (explained below).
  2. Second, you will be asked to make a guess about other people's performance (explained later).

{{Treatment}} Game

In this game, you will see boxes with 16 cells. Each cell hides a simple addition of two one-digit numbers (e.g., 1+2) and a red or black heart. Your task is to click on cells to find matching pairs based on the conditions below. For two cells to be a match two conditions must be met:

  1. the addition must be the same
  2. the hearts must be the same color
Each time you find a matching pair, the two cells will disappear. Once you find all matching pairs in one box, another box with 16 cells will appear. Each pair found counts as one problem correctly solved!

Your performance might be affected by your {{Skill}} skills.
