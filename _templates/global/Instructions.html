In this study, you will play a {{Treatment}} game. You will have 2 minutes to score as many points as you can.

{{Treatment}} Game

Game
You will see a box with 16 cells. Behind each cell, there is a simple addition of two one-digit numbers (e.g. 1+2) and a red or black heart. Your task is to find the matching pairs by clicking on the corresponding cells. For two cells to be a match two conditions must be met: first, the addition must be the same, and second, the hearts must be the same color.

Scoring points
Once you find a matching pair, these cells will disappear. Once you find all matching pairs in one box, another box with 16 cells will appear. Each pair found counts as one problem correctly solved! We expect that those with stronger {{Skill}} skills will perform better.

Payment

Your payment consists of a completion payment and a bonus payment.
Conditional on successfully completing the study, you will receive a completion payment of {{C.Completion_fee}}. In addition, you can earn a bonus payment up to {{C.Max_bonus}}. Your bonus depends on your score. For each point you score you will earn a bonus of {{C.Piece_rate}}. For instance, if you score {{C.Max_score}} points you can earn the bonus of {{C.Max_bonus}}$.
